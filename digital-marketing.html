What Is Digital Marketing

Digital marketing is an umbrella term for the marketing of products or services using digital technologies, mainly on the internet, but also including mobile phones, display advertising, and any other digital medium.

It Includes:-

1. Search Engine optimization:

SEO stands for "Search Engine Optimazation".Search engine optimization is a methodology of strategies, techniques and tactics used to increase the amount of visitors to a website by obtaining a high-ranking placement in the search results page of a search engine (SERP) -- including Google, Bing, Yahoo and other search engines.It is a subset of Internet Marketing.It is also refered as seo copyrighting.because it used search engine that deal with the text.

2. PPC – Pay Per Click:

PPC stands for pay-per-click,In which client had to pay money for every click on his ads.It is most suitable forms for increase traffic of your websites.Search engine advertising is one of the most popular forms of PPC. It allows advertisers to bid for ad placement in a search engine's sponsored links when someone searches on a keyword that is related to their business offering.An effective ppc keyword shold be relevant,exhaustic and expansive.

3.SEM – Search Engine Marketing:

SEM stand for "Search Engine Marketing". Sem is a type of internet marketing that is assosiated with the reaserch and submitting a website within a search engine to increase traffic.Sem involves seo,paid listings and other search engine services that can increase traffic of websites.It help website owner to increase traffic through paid search engine advertisement.In modern days,if you want to develop business on the Internet you need to be visible in both organic and advertised links,which means you need both SEO and SEM.

4.SSM – Social Media Marketing

SSM stand for "Social Media Marketing" which referes to the process of increase traffic of client website through social sites i.e. facebook,twitter,google+,youtube etc.Social media helps to build link to websites.Social connections may also impact the relevancy of some search results, either within a social media network or at a ‘mainstream’ search engine.

5.Email Marketing

Email Marketing is a process of sending email to the customer for advertisement purpose.It is useful to build loyalty,trust and brand awareness to the users.Transactional emails,Direct Emails,Mobile Email marketing are the different types of email marketing.If compared to standard email, direct email marketing produces higher response rate and higher average order value for e-commerce businesses.
